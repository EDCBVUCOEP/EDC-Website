E-summit

OVERVIEW
The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Pune organised 'E-Summit' under the support of the college principal Prof. Anand Bhalerao. The event was a treat to all the aspiring entrepreneurs.
EVENT DETAILS It was a full day event held on 24th Sept' 2016 starting from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. in the seminar hall of the college. Sponsored by: Dunkin Donuts, Salasar Academy Media Partners: Your Story (Inspire Innovate Ignite) Designing Partners: Mobi Wonders
Ideation Workshop by: Rohit Lalwani
Panellists: -
1.Ved Muthal
2. Arman Verma
3.Mohit Harodya
IDEATION WORKSHOP
The event started with a workshop conducted by ‘Rohit Lalwani’ on the topic “How to be an Entrepreneur? " The speech included answers to various questions like, ‘Who is an entrepreneur?’, ‘When and how to start a start-up?’, and to name a few. Then moving ahead, he explained how to work on the "Business Model Canvas. "
COMPETETION
Moving on to the second tier of the event leaded by the sponsors’ speech, we conducted a competition on preparing a ‘Business Model Canvas’. Nearly, 67 zealous teams gave a fair participation. With delicious refreshments, the competition came to an end after 2 hrs.
PANEL DISCUSSION
In the 3rd tier, the panel discussion took place. Alongside, the results were being decided upon.
PRIZE DISTRIBUTION
Great ideas from great minds flourished the seminar hall when cash prizes up to 9k were given to the winners. The first prize was awarded to the business idea of "Boney Wheels" followed by the second prize to "Rent-a-go". After the prize distribution, prof. Kadam gave a vote of thanks to all the participants, principal, panellists, and the EDC members. Last but not the least, Mr. Kadam ended his words by quoting, “Scientist can discover the world, engineer can create the world, but entrepreneurs can sustain the world.” Coming to the end of the event, certificates were distributed to each and every participant with attractive vouchers from the respective sponsors.
