INTERNSTELLAR

Internstellar
'16

OVERVIEW
The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Pune organized 'INTERNSTELLAR: 2016' under the guidance of college principal Prof. Anand Bhalerao in association with PIIC (Pune International Incubation Center) and Google Developers Group. EVENT DETAILS It was a full day event held on the 6th of February, 2016 in the seminar hall C-403 of the college. 350 students participated from different engineering, Management, commerce and arts colleges from all around Pune. A staggering sum of more than 24 startups were bought to the table. The event was structured in 3 levels. Level 1: Aptitude Test The first tier started with great enthusiasm among the students and terminated with a far strengthened seal for the next level. Level 2: Group Discussion To check the command of the participants over words and thoughts. This level went along peacefully and marks were recorded individually by the jury. Level 3: Personal Interview The panel of founders of successful start-ups searched for that instinctive potential in college students while taking a personal interview, discussing about their own startups and future engagements for it. Result As time went on and the heart beats of students paced up. It is to be noted that the event ended with 37 students getting internships in 23 Google startups from PIIC.
