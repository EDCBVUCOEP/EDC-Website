STARTUP CONCLAVE

TRANSFORM VISION INTO REALITY

STARTUP
CONCLAVE '17


Startup Conclave 2017 is an initiative by EDC [Entrepreneurship Development Cell], BVUCOEP to provide a platform for budding entrepreneurs to get an insight of the entrepreneurial ecosystem and to help them start their own venture and transform their ideas from vision to reality. Startup Conclave involves a series of sessions and talks which will guide participants to validate, ideate, and execute their venture in a sequential flow through interactive talks and Q&A sessions with experienced entrepreneurs, incubators, accelerators from the startup ecosystem. Startup Conclave is a step to spread awareness about entrepreneurship and encourage people to start their own venture. The event will include the essentials of starting up in the form of the following talks:
1. Idea Validation
2. Idea Modification
3. Prototype/Beta Version
4. Incubation
5. Funding and Acceleration
Idea Validation: Entrepreneurship is all about an idea. This talk will help participants to understand how to validate business ideas in order to determine whether or not it is worth pursuing your business venture. Idea Modification: It’s not always that your initial idea works. This talk will give participants an insight about how to modify your idea which relevant according to the market. Prototype/Beta Version: In a product based or service based startup it’s always necessary that you have a sample MVP [Minimum Value Project] or a tested service in your hand. The talk will guide people on building their first prototype/Beta version. Incubation/Funding/Acceleration: These talks will guide participants to obtain necessary resources and catalyst to scale up your venture.
